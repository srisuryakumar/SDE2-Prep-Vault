# The SDE-2 Market Reality

## Base Salary vs Total Comp (TC)
Focus on **base salary**. TC includes RSUs and bonuses which can be misleading or illiquid (unless a public global company).
For an SDE-2 in India, a **₹50 LPA base** is achievable, but it requires targeting the right companies.

## The Companies That Pay It
- **Tier 1 (Target ₹50L+ base):** Atlassian, Stripe, Coinbase, Rippling, Razorpay (top band), PhonePe (top band).
- **Tier 2 (Practice & Leverage):** Meesho, Swiggy, Groww, CRED. Use these for interview practice and to get competing offers for negotiation leverage.

## The Targeting Problem
₹50 LPA base is a *targeting* problem, not just a *talent* problem. Tier 1 companies don't hire "anyone who can clear DSA." They look for specific domain language (payments, infra, developer tooling) and candidates who bypass the generic resume pile via referrals.

## The 4-Scenario Model for Your Search
1. **Conservative (₹25–35 LPA):** Lower band Tier 1 or a Tier 2 offer. A solid win (5-7x of a 5LPA base).
2. **Expected (₹35–50 LPA):** Mid-band Tier 1, possibly with a competing Tier 2 offer.
3. **Aggressive (₹50–65 LPA):** Multiple Tier 1 offers and a strong negotiation.
4. **Best-case (₹65+ LPA):** Exceptional performance and bidding war. Treat as upside, not the baseline.

## Why Referrals Matter
Cold applications at Tier 1 compete with 200–800 others and get filtered by ATS.
Referrals route directly to a recruiter's queue. They convert to interviews at **5–10x the rate** of cold applications.
