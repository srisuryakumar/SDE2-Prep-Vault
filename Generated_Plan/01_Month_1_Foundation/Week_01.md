## Day 6 (Weekend) — Consolidate Two Pointers and Binary Search.

### DSA Block (3.5 hrs)
- Problem 1: 3Sum — LeetCode #15 — Pattern: Sorting + Two Pointers
  - Hint: Sort the array first, iterate with a fixed pointer, and use two pointers for the remaining elements to sum to zero.
  - Complexity: Time O(n²) | Space O(1)
- Problem 2: Container With Most Water — LeetCode #11 — Pattern: Two Pointers (opposite ends)

- Problem 3: Binary Search — LeetCode #704 — Pattern: Binary Search (standard)
