## How to Use This Book

**If you have 4 weeks:** Read chapters sequentially. Do one LLD design per day in weeks 3–4.

**If you have 2 weeks:** Read Chapter 1 and 13 first. Then focus on the patterns you feel weakest on and do every LLD chapter.

**If you have 3 days:** Read Chapter 13 (strategy), then skim LLD chapters 5–9 for the most common interview designs.

**The golden rule:** Every pattern exists to solve a specific pain point. Before reading the solution, read the "Problem" section and try to design a solution yourself. The struggle makes the pattern stick.

## Prerequisites

- Java 11+ syntax (records, var, switch expressions are fine)
- Basic Spring Boot knowledge (annotations, dependency injection)
- Familiarity with interfaces and abstract classes
- Comfort reading multi-class Java examples

No prior knowledge of design patterns or LLD interviews is assumed.
