First technical interview done. Here's what happened.

Company: [Tier A product company]
Round: DSA — 1 problem, 45 minutes

Problem: Given a list of meeting intervals, find the minimum number of
conference rooms required. (LeetCode #253)

What I did right:
→ Clarified: are intervals [start, end] inclusive? Are times in minutes?
→ Said the pattern out loud: "This is a heap problem — I'll use a min-heap of end times"
→ Talked through the approach before writing code (interviewer nodded)
→ Finished with 8 minutes to spare
→ Analysed: O(n log n) time, O(n) space — stated before optimisation question

What surprised me:
The interviewer asked "can you optimise the space?" — I hadn't prepared
a two-pointer approach using sorted start/end arrays. Got through it
but took 4 minutes of thinking.

Lesson: for every heap problem, know the two-pointer variant.

Result: Waiting for feedback.
