Before I negotiate any offer, here's the research I do.

Step 1: Levels.fyi
Search: [Company] [City] SDE-2 [Year]
Note: base, bonus, ESOP, total comp at P25/P50/P75

Step 2: Glassdoor
Filter: Salary, Software Engineer, [City], last 12 months
Cross-reference with Levels.fyi for consistency

Step 3: Blind
Search: "[Company] SDE2 offer" in posts
Most honest data — anonymous, often very recent

Step 4: My floor
After research, I define: minimum base I'll sign for.
Below that, I counter regardless of how excited I am.

Step 5: My anchor
Open negotiations at minimum + 15%.
If they accept immediately, I left money on the table.
If they push back, I have room to concede.

The most important rule:
Never accept verbally. Always in writing.
Always get the written offer letter before resigning.
