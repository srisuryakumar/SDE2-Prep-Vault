What I'd tell myself on Day 1.

1. Start the DSA repository immediately.
   Every problem solved, committed and pushed.
   The contribution graph is a signal. Green from Day 1.

2. Build projects with TestContainers from the start.
   Not H2. Real databases. Real bugs.

3. Write on LinkedIn as you go.
   The audience you build in Week 3 is the referral source in Week 13.

4. Find an accountability partner in Week 1.
   One mock interview per week, every week.
   The 8 mocks were worth more than 100 LeetCode problems.

5. Do not skip Sunday.
   The brain consolidates during rest. Week 1 I worked all 7 days.
   Week 2 I came back slower. Never skipped again after that.

6. The projects matter as much as the DSA.
   You need both. But a live Swagger UI URL in a GitHub README
   is worth 50 LeetCode problems in an interview impression.

7. Apply earlier than you think you should.
   I started applying at Day 60 to practice companies.
   Those interview failures taught me more than Week 5's studying.

Good luck. It is 120 days. It is hard. It is finite.
